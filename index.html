The Great Divide

Ad-Tech Competitive Landscape: Q3 2025

Q3 2025 was a story of divergence. The market clearly separated leaders from the pack, with performance driven by focused strategies in AI, Connected TV (CTV), and Retail Media. Top performers like The Trade Desk and Taboola delivered strong, double-digit growth, while competitors facing integration challenges or market headwinds showed declines.

Q3 Revenue by Company ($M)

The Trade Desk and Taboola maintain a significant scale advantage over other independent players.

Q3 Revenue Growth (YoY %)

A clear split in performance: TTD, TBLA, and MGNI show strong growth, while PUBM and NEXN face forecasted declines.

The New Battlegrounds: Where Growth is Won

Success this quarter was not uniform; it was concentrated in specific, high-growth sectors. Companies with a clear focus on Retail Media and CTV, or a strong AI-driven platform, outperformed significantly.

Criteo's Retail Media Pivot

Criteo's strategy is paying off, with its Retail Media segment (+11% ex-TAC growth) now the primary engine, offsetting its legacy business.

Magnite's CTV Core

Magnite's focus is clear: CTV is its core business, accounting for 45% of its ex-TAC contribution and growing 18% YoY.

Competitor Watchlist: Headwinds & Big Moves

Not all competitors thrived. The Outbrain/Teads merger is facing challenges, and Pubmatic faces a difficult forecast. Meanwhile, Nexxen made a critical long-term data play.

Outbrain (Teads)

Integration Headwinds

The newly merged entity reported Q3 results that "fell short of expectations" due to integration complexity.

Bright Spot:

Their standalone CTV business is a key growth area, up ~40% YoY.

Pubmatic (PUBM)

Facing Headwinds

Analysts forecast a challenging quarter (reports Nov 10) with a potential revenue decline of ~10.8% YoY.

Stated Reason:

A "DSP reset" as key partners change their buying behavior, creating near-term disruption.

Nexxen (NEXN)

The Long-Term Data Play

While Q3 forecasts are soft (reports Nov 13), Nexxen made a critical strategic move this quarter.

Key Q3 News:

Renewed & expanded its exclusive data partnership with TV maker **VIDAA** (Hisense).
